## MANDATORY SEQUENCE

### 1. Introduce Motifs

Display:
"**What visual or narrative elements repeat throughout your story?**

Motifs are the symbolic vocabulary of your production - recurring images, sounds, phrases, or colors that accumulate meaning.

Consider three types:
- **Visual Motif** - Images or objects that recur (clocks, mirrors, birds...)
- **Dialogue Motif** - Phrases or patterns in speech
- **Color Motif** - Colors that carry meaning

*Examples:*
- *'Clocks appear before death'*
- *'Characters quote the same poem at turning points'*
- *'Red appears whenever betrayal is near'*

What elements do you see repeating in your story?"

### 2. Explore Visual Motifs

Display:
"**Visual Motif:**

What image or object keeps appearing? What does it mean?

Think about:
- Objects that characters interact with repeatedly
- Environmental elements that recur (water, fire, doors...)
- Symbols that appear in different contexts

What visual element threads through your story?"

Listen and help them articulate.

### 3. Explore Dialogue Motifs

Display:
"**Dialogue Motif:**

Are there phrases, questions, or speech patterns that repeat?

Think about:
- Lines that echo across scenes or characters
- Questions that get asked multiple times
- Catchphrases or verbal tics that carry meaning

What words or phrases recur in your story?"

Listen and help them articulate. (This one may be N/A for some stories.)

### 4. Explore Color Motifs

Display:
"**Color Motif:**

Are there colors that carry specific meaning in your world?

Think about:
- Colors that appear at emotional peaks
- Color associations with characters or factions
- Shifts in color palette that signal change

What colors have meaning in your story?"

Listen and help them articulate.

### 5. Confirm Motifs

Once motifs are defined:

Display:
"**Your Recurring Motifs:**

- **Visual Motif:** {their visual motif}
- **Dialogue Motif:** {their dialogue motif or 'N/A'}
- **Color Motif:** {their color motif}

These become the symbolic language of your production. The Cinematographer will weave them consistently throughout.
