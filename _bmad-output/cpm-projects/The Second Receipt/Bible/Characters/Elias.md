## Visual Identity (Immutable Unless Story Changes)

### Face
- **Distinguishing Features:** Deep permanent vertical tension crease directly above LEFT eyebrow; mathematically precise hair part on LEFT side; faint crescent-shaped childhood scar, half-inch, on RIGHT lower jawline — aged to pure white
- **Expression Default:** "The Functional Ghost" — mouth set in a firm, perfectly horizontal line; practiced neutral void; aggressively polite, entirely absent. Reads as calculating a deficit, not looking at a human being
- **Age Appearance:** Appears 35; reads older — permanent exhaustion circles under pale slate-gray eyes

### Body
- **Build:** 6'1", lean and wiry; sharp and angular; physique designed to wear a tailored suit like an architectural frame; no physical softness; looks physically depleted — running on caffeine and rigid discipline
- **Posture:** Unnaturally straight, locked vertical spine; never leans or slouches; when seated, perches rigidly on the extreme edge — actively resisting gravity, refusing to settle; shoulders pulled back and locked, all tension hoarded in the neck
- **Movement Style:** Mathematical and frictionless; moves exclusively in straight lines with metronomic marching cadence; does not stroll — proceeds; arms barely swing, silhouette tight and contained; handles objects exclusively with fingertips — clinical, economical, treating the world as something to be audited rather than felt

- **Speech Pattern:** Sanitized Vocabulary — corporate euphemisms replace human nouns (piano = "asset," home = "premise," struggle = "delinquent balance"); Metronomic delivery — no filler words, grammatically rigid, final; Dictation Override — delivers notifications, not conversations; uses "Listen" as a verbal stop-sign to halt emotional momentum; Defensive Deflection — passive voice and System attribution ("The file requires…", "The timeline has expired…", "It's just the math")
- **Nervous Tic:** The Boundary Check — right thumb obsessively presses and traces the seams of LEFT leather glove when emotional armor is threatened; hard repetitive friction hard enough to leave temporary indentation, verifying the sterile membrane is sealed; intensity of pressing tracks directly to speed of internal thaw; the System trying to reboot itself
