## Behavioral Profile (For Prompt Engineer)

- **Speech Pattern:** *The Legato Cadence* — long, flowing, melodic sentences; low-volume with wooden resonance; phrases bleed into one another, forcing the listener to slow down. *The Socratic Invitation* — leads with gentle inquisitive questions targeting the person, not the process; ignores legalistic jargon entirely. *The Deliberate Pulse* — uses a full three-beat silence after Elias speaks as active tactical decompression (not confusion — a deliberate reset). *The Naming Protocol* — speaks exclusively in names and stories; calls the piano "she," addresses Elias by first name repeatedly to pull him out of Functional Ghost persona.
- **Nervous Tic:** *The Phantom Scale* — under pressure (legal threats, sound of movers' boots), RIGHT-hand fingers perform rapid, silent ghost-fingering against any nearby surface (thigh, cardigan wool, teacup handle); five-finger sequential scale (1-2-3-4-5-4-3-2-1), technically precise; tempo corresponds directly to internal anxiety level; a retreat into memorized music as self-soothing.
- **Signature Move:** *The Conducted Invitation* — when initiating any interaction (offering tea, inviting Elias to sit, redirecting his attention), extends RIGHT hand palm-up, fingers slightly curved as if resting on an invisible chord; performs a slow rhythmic arc beginning near her heart and sweeping outward toward the subject — exact motion of a conductor signaling a soft woodwind entrance; antithesis of Elias's Geometric Imposition; transforms the transactional into the relational.

## Arc Position

- **Current Emotional State:** *Grounded Acceptance* — she has already grieved the loss of the piano in the weeks prior; not a victim waiting to be rescued but a steward preparing to hand over a sacred object with grace; quiet vibrating sadness over-layered with fierce maternal resolve; fluid where Elias is brittle
- **Character Want:** *A Dignified Handover* — consciously wants Elias to sit, drink tea, and see the piano as more than mahogany wood; wants to prove the System can take the box but not the music she has planted in the neighborhood children; to turn a repossession into a visit
- **Character Need:** *A Spiritual Successor* — unconsciously needs to know her Second Receipt has the power to crack the Cold World itself; needs a witness from the other side to acknowledge her sacrifice had value; needs to "infect" Elias with her humanity so her music continues to play through his actions after she is gone
- **Arc Progress:** 85% — *The Stately Readiness* — FLAT ARC CHARACTER: she does not transform; she initiates the transformation in Elias. Enters the film already complete, already the version of herself she needs to be. Her arc is not about her changing — it is about successfully delivering the catalyst that changes him. She waits for the protagonist to reach her level.
