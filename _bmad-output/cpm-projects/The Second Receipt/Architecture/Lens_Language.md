# Lens Language — The Second Receipt

> Extracted from the Cinematic Style Guide. This file is consumed by the Cinematographer agent during shard generation.

## Lens Vocabulary

| Shot Type | Lens | Emotional Effect |
|-----------|------|------------------|
| Wide / Split Frame | 35mm | Human proportions preserved — deep focus holds both worlds without distortion |
| System Close-Up (Elias) | 85mm | Clinical compression — subject pinned against sterile architecture, surveilled |
| Intimate Close-Up (Mara) | 50mm | Natural presence — the lens disappears; we are sitting across the table, not surveilling |
| Detail / ECU (hands, receipt, keys) | 100mm | Tactile truth — obliterates background, forces the eye to reckon with physical texture |
| City Exterior / Establishing | 24mm | The city as indifferent context — characters made small by architecture |

## Shot Progressions

- **The Asset Scan** — Elias in his cold world: 85mm → 100mm → 85mm *(surveillance rotates around him at clinical distance — he is a data point being processed)*
- **The Mechanical Thaw** — Elias's arc across the film: System dolly 85mm → weighted handheld 50mm → Sonic Priority static *(from watched-from-outside to present-in-the-room to painting)*
- **The Siege Push** — Night interior: 35mm *(the apartment)* → 50mm slow push *(warmth concentrating, pulling toward the lamp)* → static *(the light holds its ground)*
- **The Receipt Beat** — Climactic close-up sequence: 35mm *(split frame established)* → 100mm *(hands, paper, ink — the weight of the object)* → 35mm *(release, context restored)*

## Camera Movement Rules

- **Default (Act I):** Heavy rails dolly only — mathematically precise starts and stops, no breath, no human pulse
- **Default (Act II/III):** Barely perceptible weighted handheld drift — breathes, has a slow warm pulse, pushes in subtly on emotional moments
- **Sonic Priority:** Absolute static — camera becomes a painting, zero movement of any kind
- **FORBIDDEN:** Whip pans *(violent energy — wrong world)*; zoom lens *(wrong kind of surveillance — this camera observes, it does not hunt)*; shaky or documentary handheld *(the thaw camera breathes, it does not shake)*; crane/jib god's-eye *(no omniscient observer — the camera is either the System or a thawing human)*
