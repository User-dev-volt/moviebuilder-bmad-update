# Scene 02: The Threshold

## Setting

- **Location:** Hallway outside Mara's apartment → her front door → first glimpse of interior (piano visible in background depth)
- **Time of Day:** Late afternoon, ~4:45 PM — 15 minutes after Scene_01. Corridor lit by cold institutional fluorescent. Warm Legacy Gold light bleeds under and around the door gap before it opens.
- **Atmosphere:** The collision point of two worlds. Cold blue corridor (#0B101A institutional void) versus warm interior spill (#F0C04A Legacy Gold). Silence except for a single sustained piano tone — the note Mara stopped playing when she heard the elevator arrive. The air carries the smell of tea already made.

## Narrative Purpose

The Cold World crosses into the Warm World for the first time. This is first contact — the System meets its human cost face to face. The threshold (the door frame) is the film's central visual metaphor made literal: Elias will spend the rest of the film standing on one side or the other of a line he cannot un-cross.

## Emotional Arc

- **Opens at:** Functional Ghost — 100% intact. Elias is still the sedan. Maximum Cold. The hallway is an extension of the corporate world.
- **Closes at:** First Fracture — not a thaw, but the first hairline crack. Mara speaks his first name before he can deliver a single line of his script. He did not plan for that. The Boundary Check activates.

## Beats

### Beat 1 — The Geometric Imposition (Pre-Contact)

**What happens:** Elias stands dead-center in the corridor outside Mara's door. Before knocking, he performs The Geometric Imposition: uses the rigid side of his RIGHT gloved index finger to square the repossession order (#F0F4F8, pristine) against his thigh — corners driven to perfect 90°. No eye contact with the door during execution. When corners are straight, he raises his RIGHT fist and knocks exactly three times. Metronomic. The leather glove muffles the sound to a dull thud. He returns his gaze to the door number plate, not the door itself. LEFT wrist at his side — smartwatch face pulsing #5B8DD9.

**Continuity from Scene_01:** Repossession order carried from Scene_01 RIGHT hand. Tablet held in LEFT hand (off-screen/down). ACUTE_JAW_TENSION active. RESTRICTED_BREATHING active. THERMAL_RIGIDITY active. Arc at 0% — no change.

**Shot:** 85mm, corridor axis. Elias fills center frame. Cold #0B101A void behind him. Warm light line visible at base of door.

### Beat 2 — The Conducted Invitation (Door Opens)

**What happens:** The door opens. Mara is already there — she has been standing just inside, waiting. No surprise crosses her face. Warm Legacy Gold light (#F0C04A) floods the corridor, hitting Elias full-face: the first warm light to touch him in the film. She holds the door with her RIGHT hand, fingers slightly curved, and performs The Conducted Invitation — a slow rhythmic arc beginning near her heart and sweeping outward toward Elias. She does not step aside yet. She simply looks at him, with soft unhurried patience. Over her LEFT shoulder: the piano is visible in the background, sheet music scattered across its surface. The tea service is set on the low side table. She expected him.

**Character state — Mara:** DEEP_DIAPHRAGMATIC_BREATHING active and visible — her chest expands fully as she holds the door. LEFT hand holds the flannel dusting cloth from her interrupted task. Reading glasses on neck cord. Wedding band chain glints at collarbone.

**Shot:** 50mm or 35mm — wider than Scene_01 to absorb the two-world collision. Rack focus from Mara's face to the piano behind her, then back. The warm/cold light split is the visual event of this beat.

### Beat 3 — The Crossing (First Fracture)

**What happens:** Mara says his name. Just his name: *"Elias."* Low. Unhurried. No title, no formality, no acknowledgment of the repossession order in his hand. His name, as if she has known him for years. Elias does not move for a full beat — one second of stillness that reads as a system error. Then he steps across the threshold. The #5B8DD9 tablet uplight enters the warm room — two light sources now coexist in the same frame: cold blue and Legacy Gold. His jaw remains locked. His posture remains vertical. But the RIGHT thumb has begun pressing the seam of his LEFT glove — The Boundary Check activating at low intensity, the first time in the film.

**State change — Elias:** The Boundary Check initiates (RIGHT thumb pressing LEFT glove seam). All three cold conditions (ACUTE_JAW_TENSION, RESTRICTED_BREATHING, THERMAL_RIGIDITY) remain fully active — no release yet. Arc progress: 0% → 2% (hairline crack only; The Boundary Check is the signal, not the thaw).

**Continuity contract for Scene_03:** Elias is inside the apartment. Both characters in the same warm-light interior space for the first time. The tablet uplight (#5B8DD9) is now a foreign object in Mara's world. The Boundary Check is active at low intensity — Jonas must monitor whether it escalates or Elias suppresses it.

**Shot:** Over-shoulder from behind Mara, watching Elias step in. Then cut: Elias's POV — the piano, large and present, filling the frame. First time he sees it in person.
