# Scene 01: The Functional Ghost

## Setting

- **Location:** Two chambers of the same Cold System — Elias's corporate sedan (sealed, mobile server room: Tablet Blue glow, crushed charcoal-blue shadows, no city bleed) transitioning to the apartment building hallway (institutional purgatory: cinderblock, peeling linoleum, System Green fluorescent, a single numbered door at the vanishing point)
- **Time of Day:** Late afternoon, approaching golden hour (~4:30 PM, late autumn/winter) — low weak sun, razor-sharp geometric shadows, the tipping point between artificial System light and Mara's amber warmth
- **Atmosphere:** The Synthetic Cold of Preservation — engineered, moistureless, predatory stillness. Sedan: hermetically filtered air (ozone, new leather, sterile electronics), Pressurized Vacuum soundscape (city muffled to sub-bass rumble, engine idle thrum, gloved finger tap on wheel). Hallway: neglected stagnant air (institutional floor wax, damp plaster), abrasive fluorescent buzz, shoe clack echoing on linoleum. A space deliberately designed to kill empathy before the job begins.

## Narrative Purpose

Scene 01 establishes the full weight of the Cold: Elias as a fully-operational machine, armed with a clear objective (repossess the piano) and a self-armoring ritual that winds the spring of dread — calibrating the audience to exactly what Mara's warmth will be up against when he raises his hand to knock.

**Serves theme:** The "Good Soldier" Complex (primary) — every beat is an act of self-suppression, conscience buried under "just doing my job." Transactional vs. Relational Wealth (secondary) — Sarah's voice reduces Mara's life to a file number and the piano to "mahogany wood alone."

## Emotional Arc

- **Opens at:** Sterile Isolation (Absolute Zero) — deadened, chronic exhaustion masquerading as peace; the frictionless boredom of a machine on a routine process
- **Closes at:** Pressurized Anticipation (The Coiled Spring) — Functional Ghost armor fully locked, predatory forward motion, a weapon about to be fired into someone's life

## Beats

### Shard 1 — The Asset Scan
- **Action:** Elias, face locked in the Functional Ghost expression (jaw clenched, mouth a firm horizontal line), stares downward into harsh Tablet Blue (#5B8DD9) light from off-screen. He performs a single precise swipe with his RIGHT hand — just off-screen — and the blue light on his face flickers as the file turns. He does not blink.
- **Character Focus:** Elias — 85mm clinical close-up on face; RIGHT hand off-screen
- **Emotional Note:** Absolute Zero. A machine executing a routine command. No human cost registered.

### Shard 2 — The Metronome
- **Action:** Elias's LEFT wrist rotates up into the Tablet Blue light, revealing the digital smartwatch countdown. His RIGHT gloved index finger descends and taps the glass once — cutting Sarah's voice mid-sentence. He drops his wrist out of frame and returns to absolute, symmetrical stillness.
- **Character Focus:** Elias — LEFT wrist and RIGHT gloved finger, 100mm extreme close-up
- **Emotional Note:** Dominance without anger. Sarah is not a person; she is an input to be silenced.

### Shard 3 — The Geometric Imposition
- **Action:** Elias places his matte-black tablet on the passenger seat. He pulls the Institutional Bleach repossession paper (#F0F4F8) from his RIGHT side, lays it over the tablet screen, then uses the rigid straight edge of his RIGHT gloved index finger to push the paper until its corner locks at a perfect 90-degree angle with the tablet edge. His jaw muscle twitches once at alignment.
- **Character Focus:** Elias — hands and paper, 85mm medium close-up
- **Emotional Note:** Ritual order. The world must be geometrically correct before the job begins.

### Shard 4 — The Boundary Check
- **Action:** Elias steps out of the car; the heavy door slams, plunging him into the flickering System Green light (#8FBCAA). As he begins walking, his RIGHT thumb aggressively traces the leather seam of his LEFT glove, wrist to knuckle, with heavy friction. His shoulders lock back and down; his spine snaps vertical.
- **Character Focus:** Elias — full body, 35mm tracking exterior-to-hallway
- **Emotional Note:** The armoring ritual. He is physically manufacturing the Functional Ghost before he arrives.

### Shard 5 — The March
- **Action:** Camera tracks backward on rails, perfectly matched to Elias's speed. He marches dead-center down the peeling linoleum hallway — metronomic, not strolling. Arms pinned to his sides, barely swinging. His Slate Blue silhouette compressed and narrow against the sickly green walls, boxed in by the receding lines of ceiling and floor.
- **Character Focus:** Elias — full figure, 24mm wide, system dolly
- **Emotional Note:** A man who has become the hallway. Geometric, inevitable, suffocating.

### Shard 6 — The Apex of the System
- **Action:** Elias stops with zero drift. He stands before Mara's chipped wooden door, heavily short-sided screen-left, the pristine white repossession paper held flat against his RIGHT thigh. He raises his LEFT hand, curls the leather glove into a tight rigid fist, and strikes the wood exactly three times — rapid, mathematically identical. He drops his arm to his side and freezes, eyes locked straight ahead on the grain of the door.
- **Character Focus:** Elias — 85mm clinical close-up; LEFT fist and door
- **Emotional Note:** The Apex. The system has arrived. Input delivered. Waiting for output.
